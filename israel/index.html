Trip Superlatives

The unofficial awards

Every family trip hands out a few honors. Here are this one's — decided by absolutely no one, entirely deserved.

🚆

Most dramatic 43 minutes

The high-speed climb from the coastal plain up 750 metres into the Judean Hills. Window seats non-negotiable.

🙈

Most trains missed

Officially 4+. Unofficially, we stopped counting. Includes one legendary accidental detour to Modi'in.

🕯️

Most important stop

The Nova memorial at Re'im. Not fun — never that — but the reason a trip becomes something you carry.

🧱

Best journey's-end

The whole family at the Kotel at dusk, flag overhead. Every wrong turn was worth this one right one.

🥪

MVP snack

The ILAN'S baguette + Coke Zero, consumed at altitude on the train table. Fuel for the ascent.

🧭

Guide of the week

Amler — for the roads, the stories, and knowing exactly when to let the silence do the talking.
